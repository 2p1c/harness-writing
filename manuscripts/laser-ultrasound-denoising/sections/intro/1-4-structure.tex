\paragraph{The remainder of this paper is organized as follows. Section~\ref{sec:related-work} reviews related work on laser ultrasound denoising and deep learning-based signal processing. Section~\ref{sec:methodology} describes the proposed denoising framework, including the network architecture and training strategy. Section~\ref{sec:experiment} presents the experimental setup, describing the ultrasound data acquisition, the synthetic noise models, and the evaluation metrics. Section~\ref{sec:results} reports and analyzes the quantitative results and compares the proposed method with baseline approaches. Section~\ref{sec:discussion} discusses the practical implications, limitations, and potential directions for future research. Finally, Section~\ref{sec:conclusion} summarizes the key findings and contributions of this work.}
