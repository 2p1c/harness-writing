\subsection{Limitations and Future Directions}
\label{sec:conclusion-limitations}

The method's primary limitations are the paired training data requirement and the current single-material (aluminum) scope; cross-thickness generalization remains a gap, with measurable SNR degradation on specimens outside the training thickness range. Future work should address multi-material training through domain adaptation, self-supervised denoising to reduce paired-data dependence, and real-time deployment on embedded platforms. PMDF-Net demonstrates the potential of physics-constrained multi-domain fusion for practical laser ultrasonic inspection.
