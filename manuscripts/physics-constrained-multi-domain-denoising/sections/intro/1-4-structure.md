The remainder of this paper is organized as follows. Section 2 reviews related work, covering laser ultrasonic signal characteristics, deep learning for NDT, multi-domain processing, and physics-informed neural networks. Section 3 presents the proposed PMDF-Net architecture, including the physics-constrained loss function and training scheme. Section 4 describes the experimental setup, including the paired dataset, baseline methods, and ablation configurations. Section 5 evaluates denoising performance, defect detection capability, and model generalization. Section 6 discusses implications and concludes with future directions.
